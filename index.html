Mindful Peer – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Owner: Frank Blackman Jr.

Contact: frankblackman12@gmail.com


1. Introduction

Mindful Peer (“the App”) is a mental wellness tool designed to help users manage journaling, daily tasks, and emotional check-ins. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected.

2. Information We Collect

The only personal information collected is an email and password required to create an account using Firebase Authentication. No other information is collected.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No name
  • No date of birth
  • No location
  • No contacts
  • No photos
  • No device identifiers
  • No tracking data

4. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used only for account creation, login, and password reset. We do not sell or share your data.

5. Children's Privacy

Mindful Peer may be used by children under 13. We collect only an email and password for login and do not collect any other personal data. Parents may request deletion at any time.

6. Data Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which uses industry-standard encryption and complies with global privacy regulations.

7. Data Deletion Requests

To delete your data, contact frankblackman12@gmail.com. Requests are processed within 48 hours.

8. Contact Us

Email us at frankblackman12@gmail.com for any privacy-related concerns.
